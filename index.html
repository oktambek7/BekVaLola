Project Overview

Built for Uzbek children

Bek & Lola is an interactive, curriculum-aligned educational game designed for Uzbek-speaking children aged 3–8. Players learn the Uzbek alphabet, numbers, shapes, colors, animals, and everyday skills through engaging characters and mini-games.

The game reflects local culture and language, filling a gap in Uzbek-language digital educational content. Analytics are used to track learning engagement, session quality, and user retention across the player base.

Target Market

Uzbekistan · Uzbek-speaking diaspora · Central Asia

Product Highlights

What the game teaches

Core learning pillars that drive engagement and retention in the Bek & Lola experience.

Uzbek Alphabet

Interactive letter recognition, pronunciation, and writing exercises tailored to the Uzbek Latin script.

Numbers & Counting

Visual counting games that build numeracy skills for children from early pre-school to first grade.

Animals & Nature

Illustrated quizzes and soundboards introducing animals, plants, and natural environments.

Shapes & Colors

Pattern recognition and color identification games that strengthen cognitive foundational skills.

Everyday Life Skills

Scenarios based on daily routines — morning habits, meals, clothing — taught through story-driven play.

Songs & Audio

Original Uzbek-language songs and rhymes that reinforce vocabulary through auditory learning.

Product & Data Analytics Role

I designed and implemented the full analytics pipeline for Bek & Lola — from event schema design and data collection through to the interactive Power BI reporting layer that gives the product team actionable insight into player behavior and game performance.

  • Designed the analytics event schema and integrated Firebase tracking across all game modules
  • Built the Power BI dashboard with real-time data connections, covering DAU/WAU/MAU, session depth, and retention cohorts
  • Defined KPI frameworks to measure learning engagement and feature adoption across age groups
  • Presented insights to the product team to inform roadmap prioritization and content updates
  • Collaborated with developers to instrument new features and A/B test content variations
